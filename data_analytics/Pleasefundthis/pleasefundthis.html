Key Insights

Record-Breaking Interest

More people signed up for library cards in 2023 than at any point in the last decade.

The "Space" Factor

The larger a branch's physical size is the more people use every service it offers.

Digital Hubs

High correlation between workstation usage and registrations. While people are borrowing fewer physical books, they are increasingly using libraries as offices and tech centers.

A "Top-Heavy" System

A Reference Libraries handle a huge portion of the city's total traffic, while many neighborhood branches see significantly less engagement.
